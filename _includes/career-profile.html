Introduction

I am a PhD student in CSTR at the University of Edinburgh. I work on neural sequence-to-sequence modelling of text-to-speech with a particular interest in pronunciation modelling. My research is funded by the ESRC and my supervisors are Korin Richmond and Steve Renals.

Teaching
