Pronunciation Mining

I am a PhD student in CSTR at the University of Edinburgh. I work on mining word pronunciations from large datasets of audio to construct lexica for use in Text-to-Speech (TTS) synthesis and Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR). My research is funded by the ESRC and my supervisors are Korin Richmond and Steve Renals.

Hate Speech Detection

For my MSc dissertation I studied Hate Speech Detection on Twitter (supervised by Bonnie Webber and Liane Guillou).
